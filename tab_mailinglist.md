## Signing up the mailing list

This is now even more important since OWASP is moving away from meetup.com

### OPTION A - Sending an email

Probably the easiest option

- Step 1) Email <a href="mailto:israel-chapter+subscribe@owasp.org" target="_blank">israel-chapter+subscribe@owasp.org</a>.
- Step 2) You will then receive an email offering to let you click a "Join this Group" button or reply to the email. Doing either of these two things will complete your subscription to the mailing list.

### OPTION B

Slightly less easy but you can also do this via the Google Groups Website

- Step 1) Browse the Google Groups website: <a href="https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/g/israel-chapter" target="_blank">https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/g/israel-chapter</a>
- Step 2) Click "Join Group"
- Step 3) Choose subscription "Each email"
- Step 4) Press "Join group" to complete your subscription to the mailing list.
